The History of Tea and Coffee Around the World

Tea and coffee have been cherished for centuries — from ancient Chinese tea ceremonies to Ethiopian coffee legends. Tea became a symbol of peace and wellness, while coffee fueled creativity and conversation in cafés across the world.

At Gulch, we honor this rich heritage by sourcing the finest tea leaves and coffee beans from Kenya. Each cup we offer blends tradition, quality, and the warmth of shared moments.
